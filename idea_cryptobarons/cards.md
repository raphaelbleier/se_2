# Card Definitions – Crypto Barons

## Card System Overview

Each player starts with a hand of **5 cards**. Hands are replenished to 5 at the start of each
Lobbying Phase. Cards are drawn from a shared shuffled deck.

Players may play **up to 2 cards per turn** during the Lobbying Phase.

---

## Card Catalogue

---

### Card 1: "3AM Capslock Tweet"

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Type** | Market Manipulation |
| **Phase** | Lobbying Phase |
| **Rarity** | Uncommon |
| **Cost** | 0 FD |

**Effect:**
Select one crypto asset (PatriotCoin or LibertyDoge). That asset's value is multiplied by **5x**
for the **current round only**.

At the **end of the Audit Phase**, the asset **crashes back to 10% of its pre-card value**.

**Interaction with Insider Trading:**
Any player who purchased the targeted asset in the **current or previous Lobbying Phase** (before
the card was played) profits massively from the spike before the crash. This is traceable in the
audit log.

**AI Trigger:**
Triggers the "Irrational Politician" AI persona to generate a social feed post hyping the asset.

**Satirical Flavor:**
> *"Just heard about this coin at 3AM. TREMENDOUS coin. Maybe the best coin. People are saying it.
> BUY NOW before the DEEP STATE shorts it!"*

### Card 2: "Alternative Facts"

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Type** | Market Manipulation |
| **Phase** | Lobbying Phase |
| **Rarity** | Rare |
| **Cost** | 0 FD |

**Effect:**
Inverts the percentage market change of **all assets** from the previous round.
- Assets that fell 20% last round now retroactively "rose" 20% for FD calculation purposes
- Losses in the player's portfolio from the previous round are converted to equivalent gains
- Does **not** affect other players' portfolios

**Duration:** One-time effect, applies retroactively to last round's results for the card user only.

**AI Trigger:**
Triggers "Irrational Politician" persona: a post claiming the market crash was fabricated.

**Satirical Flavor:**
> *"The numbers you saw? Fake. My ACTUAL portfolio is up 40%. The LAMESTREAM financial press
> won't report it. SAD!"*

### Card 3: "Nepotism Board Member"

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Type** | Political Corruption |
| **Phase** | Lobbying Phase |
| **Rarity** | Rare |
| **Cost** | 100,000,000 FD (one-time placement fee) |

**Effect (Persistent, Until End of Game):**

**Removal Condition:**

**AI Trigger:**

**Satirical Flavor:**
> *"Congratulations to [Player Name]'s family member on their appointment to the Department of

### Card 4: "The Ultimate Rug Pull"

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Type** | Advanced Fraud / Market Manipulation |
| **Phase** | Lobbying Phase (multi-round setup) |
| **Rarity** | Legendary |
| **Cost** | 500,000,000 FD (token issuance fee) |

**Setup Phase (Round N):**
1. Player plays this card and pays 500,000,000 FD
2. A custom token "[$PLAYERNAME]Coin" is created and listed on the in-game market
3. Initial token price: 1,000 FD per unit
4. All other players receive a mandatory notification: *"New investment opportunity!"*

**Forced Investment Mechanic (Round N to N+2):**
- Each other player must invest between 10,000,000 and 100,000,000 FD into the token
  (exact amount randomized by server) or forfeit their next turn
- The token's price is artificially inflated +50% per round while the setup is active

**Rug Pull Execution (Round N+3 or earlier, player's choice):**
1. Player activates the pull during any Lobbying Phase
2. All FD in the token's liquidity pool is transferred to the issuing player's account
3. Token value drops to **0 FD** instantly
4. All investors lose their entire investment
5. AI generates a post from a "Regulatory Authority" persona justifying the collapse

**Leaderboard Impact:** Contributes to "Biggest Rug Pull" leaderboard entry.

**Satirical Flavor:**
> *"TECHNICAL DIFFICULTIES. The $[PLAYERNAME]Coin smart contract has been upgraded for security
> reasons. Funds are SAFU. Please ignore the empty wallet."*

### Card 5: "Presidential Pardon"

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Type** | Immunity / Defense |
| **Phase** | Audit Phase (reactive) |
| **Rarity** | Very Rare |
| **Cost** | 0 FD |

**Effect:**
When a Congressional Hearing is successfully initiated against you, you may play this card
**immediately** (within the 5-second window) instead of completing biometric authentication.

- All negative consequences of the hearing are **nullified**
- No asset confiscation
- No need to authenticate
- The accuser does **not** receive the "Fake News" penalty either — the case is simply dismissed
- The card is **consumed** upon use

**Stacking:** If a player holds multiple Pardons, they each apply to separate hearings.

**Leaderboard Impact:** Each use contributes to "Most Pardons Used" leaderboard entry.

**Satirical Flavor:**
> *"This investigation is a witch hunt. The defendant, who did nothing wrong, hereby accepts
> a full and complete pardon. Case dismissed. Nobody knows more about pardons than me."*

### Card 6: "Suppress the Post"

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Type** | Censorship / Countermeasure |
| **Phase** | Lobbying Phase (reactive) |
| **Rarity** | Uncommon |
| **Cost** | 0 FD |

**Effect:**
Target one player's FreedomFeed post submitted this Lobbying Phase.

The targeted post is **removed from the feed** and its market impact is **nullified** — the
sentiment multiplier is zeroed out before the price update at phase end.

The targeted player is notified with: *"Your post has been removed for violating Community
Standards."* (no FD penalty for the poster, but their market manipulation attempt fails)

**Stacking:** If the targeted post was a Verified post, the Suppression still works — but costs
**2 copies** of this card instead of 1.

**Satirical Flavor:**
> *"Congratulations! Your post has been reviewed and found to violate our policies on
> coordinated inauthentic behavior. We take the integrity of our platform very seriously."*

---

### Card 7: "Echo Chamber"

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Type** | Social Amplification |
| **Phase** | Lobbying Phase |
| **Rarity** | Rare |
| **Cost** | 0 FD |

**Effect:**
Select one FreedomFeed post from the current round (by any player or AI).
That post's market impact multiplier is **tripled** for the price calculation at the end of
this Lobbying Phase.

Can be played on your own post (post → ×3 impact) or on an AI persona post (free amplification
with no cost — the AI "goes viral").

**Interaction:** If played on an opponent's bearish post about your asset, it dramatically
accelerates the price drop. Best used on your own bullish post.

**Satirical Flavor:**
> *"The algorithm has decided this post deserves to reach 40 million accounts.
> Engagement is up 8,400%. Advertisers are thrilled."*
